# Treasure Hunt

How to find the Docs of MongoDB in the massive jungle of MongoDB Resouces

- Go to the [MongoDB Homepage](https://www.mongodb.com/)
- Look onto the Navbar
- Click on `Resources Dropdown Option`
- Click onto the `Documentation`
- Page Loading... Click onto the `CLIENT LIBRARIES` form the `left sidebar`
- Page Loading... `Second Sidebar Appears`
- Click on the `Node.js`
- Page Loading... `Note: most of the things are same AF`
- Click on `Node.js Drivers` under the same `Node.js` Option which is on the `Second Sidebar`
- Page Loading... `Note: almost the same structure and the same sidebar but now its different`
- Click on the `CRUD Operations` on the `Second Sidebar`

> And there it is... You're Welcome.
