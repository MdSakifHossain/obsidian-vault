# Paragraph: A Rainy Day

## Questions for paragraph on 'A Rainy Day'

1. What is a rainy day?
2. What happens on a rainy day?
3. What happens to roads, fields, tanks, etc?
4. What troubles does it bring to the people?
5. How do school boys and girls feel on a rainy day?
6. What are the troubles for the cattle?
7. What happens to the poor?

## Answer

### A Rainy Day

A rainy day is a day when it rains all day long. On a rainy day, the sky looks gloomy and is covered with clouds. The day itself is also gloomy. On a rainy day, it often rains heavily. Roads, fields, tanks, etc. go under water. Sometimes, it causes destruction. Our valuable properties are destroyed. Lands go under water, Crops are washed away. Plants and trees can be uprooted. Roads and highways are damaged. Most of the school boys and girls stay at home. So, they feel very happy. Cattle remain in their sheds. On a rainy day, the poor suffer more than the rich. They cannot go out for work. As a result, they have to starve. So, a rainy day is not always welcoming to all.
